Explain Descriptive analytics?
---> . Descriptive analytics involves analyzing historical data to understand what has happened in the past
     . Its primary goal is to provide a clear and concise overview of data to support decision-making and inform future actions.
     . Example - analyzing website traffic data to determine peak visit times throughout the day.


Explain Predictive analytics?
---> . Predictive analysis is the process of using data, statistical algorithms, and machine learning techniques to predict future outcomes based on historical data.
